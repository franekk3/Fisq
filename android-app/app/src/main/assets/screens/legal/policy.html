Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

1. Introduction

Fisq! (“the Service”) is an open‑source web application that provides free online flashcards. The project is licensed under the GNU General Public License v3.0 (GPLv3). Your privacy is important, and this Privacy Policy explains what data is collected, how it is stored, and how it is used.

2. Data Collection

Fisq! does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data you create or interact with — including flashcards, settings, and progress — is stored locally in your web browser using technologies such as LocalStorage or IndexedDB.

The Service does not:

  • send data to any external servers
  • use analytics tools
  • use cookies for tracking
  • require account creation or login

3. Local Data Storage

All information is stored exclusively on your device. You may delete your data at any time by clearing your browser storage or using built‑in reset options (if available).

Because data is stored locally:

  • uninstalling or clearing your browser may permanently delete your flashcards
  • your data is not accessible to the developer or third parties

4. Third‑Party Services

Fisq! does not integrate with any third‑party services, APIs, analytics, or advertising networks.

5. Open‑Source Code

Fisq! is open‑source software released under the GPLv3 license. Anyone may inspect the source code to verify how data is handled.

6. Children’s Privacy

Fisq! does not collect personal data and may be used by individuals of any age. However, children should use the Service with guidance appropriate to their region’s laws.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact

If you have questions or suggestions regarding this Privacy Policy, you may open an issue in the project’s repository.
